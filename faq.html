HOW CAN WE HELP?

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our products and services.

Ordering at ZenWares is simple! Once you find an item you like, select your size and click "Add to Cart". When you're ready to complete your purchase, go to your shopping bag and click "Checkout". We'll guide you through the shipping and payment process.

Orders within Dhaka typically arrive within 2-3 business days. For orders outside Dhaka, please allow 3-5 business days. You can track your order status in real-time using our Tracking page.

We offer a 7-day exchange policy for unused items with original tags. If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact our support team immediately for a replacement or full refund.

We have a detailed size chart available on every product page. If you're still unsure, feel free to visit any of our 60+ retail outlets across Bangladesh for a trial fitting.
