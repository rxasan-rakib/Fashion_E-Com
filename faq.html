FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to shipping, return policies, and common account queries.

We offer flat shipping rates across Bangladesh. Inside Dhaka city, the shipping fee is ৳60 and delivery takes 2-3 business days. For orders outside Dhaka, the shipping fee is ৳120 and delivery takes 3-5 business days. Free shipping is automatically applied to orders over ৳5000.

We offer a 30-day easy return and exchange policy for all unworn, unwashed items in their original packaging with tags attached. You can initiate a return by contacting our customer care or visiting our physical store at Bashundhara City.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a SMS tracking code. You can navigate to our "Track Order" page in the navigation menu, enter your Order ID or tracking code, and instantly see the real-time status of your parcel.

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) nationwide, mobile financial services (bKash, Nagad, Rocket), and secure online payments using major Visa, Mastercard, or AMEX cards processed through SSLCommerz.
